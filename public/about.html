About


NOK HISTORY



NOK is an architectural design and 3d visualization studio with artistic-led and experienced team, who helps individuals, firms and organizations to actualize their proposed design visualization to the client's amazement. Our team provides architectural imagery and animation with dedication and elegance, following unwritten rule of our crafts in a remarkable style.

BRIEF HISTORY NOK Studio was founded in March 2017, by group of architectural student of Nnamdi Azikwe University, Awka, Anambra State, in an attempt to assist students with their design approach and provide guidance along their thought process.

Nok studio blew to a professional design studio in February 2019, offering services in the fields of architectural design, interior design and landscaping This encompasses drafting, design, 3d visualization, 3d animation, 3d rendering, construction drawings and scaled modelling

However, we are passionate about bridging the gap between firms and their now increasing need for a trusted assistance in developing and bringing to life a modern evergreen design which will be pleasing to the client within an agreed ample of time and standard

Experience
Our team during the years of active practice having being opportune to work for different individuals, organizations and firms, putting into consideration the evolving nature of architecture, the trending choice and the knowledge of the fading designs.
We charge ourselves to research on the different choices that are innovative, pocket friendly and satisfies the three qualities of good design

Mission, Vision and Value


MISSION
We want to see what you see, create what you imagined and visualize the dream.

VISION STATEMENT
Our vision is to build a community of architects, who will be readily available to provide visualization assistance through shared ideas and approach towards achieving our mission.

VALUE AND PHYLOSOPHY
Our team is dedicated to bringing life to our client's imagination within the agreed time frame through discipline, research, shared ideas and good time management
